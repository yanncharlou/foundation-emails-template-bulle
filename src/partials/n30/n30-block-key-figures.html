Key figures concerning Italian products in France
Italy’s rich culinary heritage is very appreciated in France.
It is the 5th supplier country for foodstuffs in France: €4.15 million (7.8% of the French import market).
The three leading product categories are:
  • Sweet and bakery products,
  • Dairy products
  • Pasta.

+16% for sweet products
+13% for dairy products
+5.8% for pasta

Italy is the largest producer of organic products in the European Union.
